Completing an MSc in Computing

I am currently undertaking an MSc in Computing at Cardiff University as part of a career change process into Software Engineering. I am making this move as I have been looking for a field of work where I can use my natural skillset in mathematics and logic more. Prior to making this change, I worked in the charity sector for twelve years, through which I hold a breadth of experience in running projects, managing teams and budgets, and bringing about change within complex organisations. I am highly organised with a strong focus on achieving outcomes balanced with an ability to build positive relationships with a broad range of people.
